The Best Movies or Web Series according to Nikhil

My Top 5 best movies & Web series of all time

Panchayat(Web Series)

This web series is full of comedy,drama and thrilling moments.It tells about the village Phulera where Abhishek, a boy who is the Sachiv, manages his duty while also preparing for CAT.

Mirzapur(Web Series)

This web series is full of action,gun fighting and suspense events which will thrill the heart to the core. It is about the tripathi family, who rule Mirzapur and shows how people fight the Tripathis to take over the throne of Mirzapur.

3 Idiots(Movie)

This movie is a must watch for all students as it provide huge motivation to be dedicated towards what your passion is. It tells the story of a boy Rancho(real name Phunsukh Wangdu) who due to his interest in engineering,joins the Imperial College of Engineering from where he graduates as an inventor.
